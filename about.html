About Acacia Primary and Pre-Primary School

Acacia's Senior Principle: Ms. Masego Onkemetse Kemong

At Acacia Primary & Pre School, our mission is to provide the best environment that prepares students to function as Entrepreneurs, Workers, and Citizens in a Globalised World. We restore the promise of full academic enrichment to students with learning differences. We proudly say that Acacia Primary School is one of the best Private primary schools in Jwaneng Botswana.

A Taste of Our History

Acacia Primary School, located in Jwaneng(Botswana), started its operations in 1979 with a total 770 students enrolled, and 58 teaching staff members consisting of 49 citizens and 9 non-citizens. It is a private school developed and run under the Debswana Jwaneng Mine to serve the children of their workers and the overall community. The establishment of the school was also used as a strategic approach, assisted in attracting and retaining mine employees as parents would be forced to send off their children to boarding schools, or even leave their jobs and relocate to different areas where they can gain employment while their children could receive good education in the same town.
