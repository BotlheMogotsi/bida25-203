Independance Day 2025

Acacia School celebrated Botswana Independence Day with the theme "Botswana Wa Rona, Boipuso Jwa Rona – A Future Built Together." The event was vibrant and full of energy as learners showcased their talents through plays, traditional dances, and Setswana songs. Some performances were also presented in various languages, reflecting Botswana's diversity and highlighting the spirit of unity that independence represents.

Standard 6 Innovation Trip

Seventy Standard 6 pupils from Acacia Primary School recently participated in an inspiring journey that combined aviation, technology, and leadership. This second annual trip, organized in collaboration with the Dare to Dream Foundation and supported by Liquid Intelligent Technologies, Air Botswana, and parents, aimed to spark curiosity and nurture future innovators. The students experienced their first flight over Gaborone, symbolizing their potential to reach new heights. The flight, led by the German Ambassador H.E. Ms. Gabriela Renate Bennemann, was complemented by robotics and coding workshops at the Botswana Innovation Hub, fostering creativity and teamwork.

Standard 5-7 STEM Trip 2023

In 2018, Acacia Primary School, in collaboration with the Information Management (IM) Department, participated in Africa Code, training 200 students from Standard 4 to 6 and 25 teachers in digital literacy using Scratch programming. Building on this, Acacia then integrated coding into the curriculum for Standard 5 to 7 and established a Coding and Robotics Club. Additionally, the School engaged students in coding camps, the "Hour of Code" initiative, and the FLY TO STEAME programme, providing continuous exposure to Science, Technology, Engineering and Mathematics (STEM) education across the school, throughout the year and indeed to date.

Entrepreneurship Day 2022

Acacia Primary School in collaboration with The Neo Hub held an entrepreneurship day for students , under the theme ‘Enabling Botswana’s Future Entrepreneurs today’. The event created a platform for students to develop products and services that they believed their target market would like, develop a branding and marketing strategy as well as financial record keeping ensuring that they indeed made a profit.
